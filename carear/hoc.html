As the Head of Communications you will lead all aspects of Chanjelly.com’s PR and communication strategy and execution, in alignment with our broader company goals. WHAT YOU WILL NEED:
➡️ 10+ years of experience using strategic communications and public relations to create a differentiated brand. ➡️ Experience building and executing media campaigns, crisis management plans, and advising C-suite leadership. ➡️ Proven ability to create and utilize strong media and key influencer relationships to drive results; experience working with journalists at key outlets across print, broadcast and online channels. ➡️ Love of the written word and keen attention to detail (the kind of person who has strong opinions on the use of the Oxford comma and frequently identifies typos in newspapers and novels). ➡️ Initiative; ability to learn new skills and information quickly, and to work efficiently and effectively in a fast-paced environment.
