HOW YOUR WALLET WORKS

Your Wallet

When you produce your Blockchain wallet, a unique master seed phrase is made. This master seed phrase is that the centre focus of your specific wallets security, and is used in conjunction with all of your transactions.

Client Side Encryption

We don't store your bitcoins; we simply give you a safe software package from which to store them yourself. Your wallet is encrypted on your device along with your personal security word. This word acts as your cryptography key - to unlock your private access – both your wallet and funds cannot be accessed without this.

We’ve Got Your Back(up)

Your encrypted wallet is saved to our servers; to add extra security we tend to add additional layers of encryption - firmly storing your wallet on our servers. Ensuring that you can access your wallet simply and safely from multiple devices.

Decrypting your wallet couldn’t be easier

When you access your wallet along with your Wallet ID using security tokens (distinctive phrases specific to your wallet) and positive identification match, your browser can then use our API to transfer your encrypted wallet backup, before safely decrypting it on your own selected device.

Your Backup Phrase - The Failsafe

Your Backup Phrase is formed using twelve random words, connected to this is also an encoded version of your master seed which also acts as a backup, however in the event that you have any issues accessing your wallet, your Backup Phrase is all you actually need regain access to your funds via our customer service department, which requires some security checks first.

GOOD TO KNOW
